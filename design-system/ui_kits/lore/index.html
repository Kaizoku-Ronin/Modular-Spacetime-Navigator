Physics of the Simulator

Special Relativity

The flight simulator implements exact special relativistic effects. As your velocity approaches the speed of light, three phenomena become visible: aberration (stars crowd in front of you), Doppler shifting (ahead blueshift, behind redshift), and relativistic beaming.

Gravity & The Black Hole

Each system centers on a black hole whose mass is proportional to the host star. Gravity bends your heading but preserves cruise speed. The Horizon Shield prevents you from crossing the point of no return.
